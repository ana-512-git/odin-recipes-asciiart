Description:

An easy way to make pizza, including the dough. No resting needed.

Ingredients:

  • greek yoghurt
  • flour
  • tomato sauce
  • mozzarella cheese
  • whatever toppings you like

Steps:

  1. Combine the greek yoghurt with the flour until you reach a dough consistency
  2. Roll dough as thinly as possible, then transfer into pan
  3. Add toppings, then cook until the crust is slightly burnt
  4. Enjoy!
