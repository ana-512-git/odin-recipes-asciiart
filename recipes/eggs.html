Description:

This is exactly what you think it is. I'm just explaining something you likely already know=)))))

Ingredients:

  • eggs
  • bacon
  • salt
  • pepper
  • optionally, you can add some paprika or chilli flakes to the eggs

Steps:

  1. In an oiled pan, fry your bacon until crispy
  2. In the same pan, crack your eggs. As they cook, using a spoon, scoop up the oil and pour it over the eggs
  3. When almost done, add spices
  4. That's all. Enjoy!
