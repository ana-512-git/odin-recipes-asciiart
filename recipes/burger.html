Description

Fast, easy, tasty, a great way to make sure you respect your bulking regime. Prep time is about 20 minutes tops, depends on how thick you fry your burger patty.

Ingredients:

  • 500 g ground beef
  • 1-2 leaves iceberg lettuce
  • 1-2 tomato slices
  • 1 burger bun
  • 1 slice cheddar cheese
  • 2 tbsp mayo
  • 1 tbsp mustard
  • 1 pickle
  • 2 tbsp ketchup
  • optional: chilli powder

Steps:

  1. Prepare the beef by adding salt, pepper, paprika, onion powder and garlic powder. Form the meat into balls about the size of your fist
  2. In a hot oiled pan, place your beef patties and smash them as thinly as you can. Leave for 3 minutes, then flip
  3. Place a cheese slice on the fried size and wait until it melts
  4. While the meat cooks, mix the mayo, mustard, ketchup and chopped pickle to make a perfect burger sauce
  5. Optionally, you can toast your buns
  6. Place the lettuce on the bottom half of the bun, then your patties, the tomato, put some sauce on the other bun and put together
  7. Enjoy!
