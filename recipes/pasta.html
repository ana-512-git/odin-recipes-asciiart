Description:

Creamy, cheesy, delicious pasta. All you need is a few ingredients that are totally customizable and about 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • pasta
  • olive oil
  • garlic
  • tomatoes
  • cheese (any kind will likely do)

Steps:

  1. In a pot, cook pasta as instructed on the packaging
  2. In a pan, put the olive oil and while it heats, peel and mince the garlic, then add to the pan
  3. Cut the tomato, then place it in the pan
  4. Cover with a lid and leave until tomatoes are soft enough to break down when pressed with a spoon
  5. Smash the tomatoes into a sauce
  6. Add salt and basil
  7. Add cheese, some pasta water and the pasta
