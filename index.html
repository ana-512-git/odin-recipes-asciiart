In an attempt to better my web development portfolio, I've decided to take up this course, and as I have a little bit of previous experience, I've decided to put a twist on this rather simple assignment. This "homework" was intended for people with no previous experience whatsoever, so I thought I'd try to challenge myself to create a website that is still good looking in spite of having zero lines of CSS.

Now, you may ask "Is it responsive?" Nope, you can't really do that without CSS as far as I know. "Is it a cool way to explore ASCII art?" For sure. In fact, apart from the fonts, everything you see in this website is made entirely by me. And I have to admit it's a lot of fun to draw in ASCII. Don't believe me? Try for yourself on this website, where I've created the better part of the art you'll see here.
